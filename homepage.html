What We Do

Venue Recommendation

Finding the perfect venue has never been easier. Simply provide your budget, location, and guest count, and our AI-powered system will curate a list of wedding halls and venues that match your preferences.

Vendor Recommendation

Connect with the best vendors in the industry, including decorators, caterers, photographers, and more. Our platform matches you with reliable vendors that fit your budget and style, ensuring your big day is handled by the best professionals.

3D Visualization

Bring your wedding vision to life with our interactive 3D visualization tool. Powered by Blender and Three.js, this feature allows you to explore and customize decor setups in real-time, giving you a clear preview of how your wedding venue will look on the big day.
