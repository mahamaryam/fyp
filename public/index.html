About Us

Wedding Wiz is an innovative web application designed to make your wedding planning experience seamless and unforgettable. Created by a team of four talented students for their final year project (FYP), Wedding Wiz leverages cutting-edge technology to assist couples in organizing the perfect wedding. The application provides personalized vendor recommendations, ensuring that you find the best service providers for every aspect of your big day. With its unique 3D modeling feature, Wedding Wiz allows you to visualize your wedding setup, helping you make the best decisions for the ceremony and reception. Additionally, the AI-powered venue recommendations guide you in choosing the ideal location, perfectly suited to your style and preferences. This remarkable web app combines creativity, functionality, and the power of AI, making it a true game-changer in wedding planning. Wedding Wiz is a testament to the ingenuity and dedication of its creators, and it's sure to make your wedding day dreams come true!
