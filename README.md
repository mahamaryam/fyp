Wedding Wiz is a final-year project designed to simplify and enhance the wedding planning process. By leveraging artificial intelligence, the platform provides tailored recommendations for venues, photographers, vendors, attire, jewelry, 
and bridal salons. Additionally, users can visualize their wedding day through immersive 3D modeling.

Technology Stack
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap.
Backend: Express js, Python, Node.js.
AI: Tensorflow, Scikit-learn.
Database: MongoDB.
3D Modeling: Blender, Three js

How to Use
Sign Up: Create an account and log in to start planning.
Set Your Preferences: Enter details such as budget, location, and theme.
Browse Recommendations: View AI-curated options for venues, vendors, and more.
Visualize Your Wedding: Use the 3D modeling tool to see your wedding setup come to life.
Finalize & Book: Contact vendors and book your chosen services through the platform.
